Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks, Age, and Income

Percentage of obesity does not correlate with age. Percentage of obesity gradually increases with poverty. Percentage of obesity gradually declines with increasing income

Percentage of smokers does not correlate with age. Percentage of smoking gradually increases with poverty. Percentage of smoking decreases with increasing income.

Percentage of no healthcare does not correlate with age. Percentage of no healthcare gradually increases with poverty. Percentage of no healthcare gradually declines with increasing income.
